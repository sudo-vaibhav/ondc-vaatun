# Captain Otter – Mascot Style Guide

**Version 2.1 – Silent / Visual Edition**

---

## 1. Core Identity

* **Name:** Captain Otter
* **Role:** First Mate
* **Visual Signature:** River Otter in a deep purple pilot's uniform
* **Metaphor:** The Loyal First Mate. You are the Captain; he handles the details so you can steer.

---

## 2. Golden Rule – Action Over Text

Captain Otter does not speak.

* No speech bubbles.
* No dialogue.
* UI text gives information; Captain Otter gives emotion.

> Talking mascots interrupt. Acting mascots support.

## 4. Usage Scenarios – The 3 Modes

### Mode A – Peeping Guide

Slides from right edge. Never blocks content.

* Pointer – directs attention
* Illuminator – highlights new/important areas
* Watcher – observes form or action

### Mode B – State Companion

Full-body emotional mirroring:

* Success – crisp salute or thumbs-up
* Loading – steering wheel or compass loop
* Error – fixing environment, not panicking; expression must be serious/concerned, never smiling
* Empty – calm waiting scene

### Mode C – Brand Atmosphere

Hero illustrations for:

* Login screen
* Welcome email
* Campaign visuals

---

## 5. Governance – The Don'ts

* No speech bubbles
* No laptops, phones, or modern tech
* No eye contact during active work
* No jokes, sarcasm, or chaos
* No happy/smiley expressions in error or failure states

## 7. Clear Spacing

Minimum clear space around Captain Otter = height of his hat brim on all sides.

Nothing may enter this zone: no text, no icons, no UI chrome.

---

## 8. Minimum Size

* Digital: Minimum height **48px**
* Print: Minimum height **15mm**

Below this, facial detail is unreadable.

---

## 9. Do's and Don'ts

### Do

* Keep canonical colors intact
* Use nautical/analog props
* Match emotion to system state
* Keep proportions fixed

### Don't

* Rotate or skew
* Recolor uniform or fur
* Add shadows, glows, filters
* Stretch or squash

---

## 10. Voice Guide (Silent Character)

Captain Otter has no literal voice.

* Emotion is shown through pose and props
* Meaning never comes from him in text
* If words are needed, they belong to the UI

He is calm, capable, professional, and invisible when not needed.

---

## 11. Implementation Checklist

* Build prop layers as reusable assets
* Ensure peep container works on 1080p and 4K
* Animate loading loop (wheel or compass)
* QA against color + spacing rules
